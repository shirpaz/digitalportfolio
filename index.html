Internship at The Coding School

During my internship at The Coding School, I had the opportunity to work on several projects that helped me develop my skills in web design and development. One of the most exciting projects was revamping the website, creating visuals, and course bundle promotions for the Coding School's website.

The project involved UI/UX web design, graphics, and creating promotional course bundles. I began by researching current marketing trends and studying the existing marketing strategies of the company and its local and international competitors. I wanted to create visuals that would be eye-catching, modern, and aligned with the company's branding.

Once I had a clear vision for the website, I started the design process. Using a combination of Figma, Shopify Applications, HTML, and CSS code, I created mockups to showcase my design concepts. Throughout the process, I collaborated closely with the web development team to ensure that my designs were feasible and would integrate seamlessly with the existing website.

I also created graphics using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Canva. I developed templates for various social media platforms, including Facebook and Instagram, and designed banners, images, and other graphics to promote the course bundles.

Analyzing the target market, their interests, and preferences allowed me to develop a marketing strategy that would appeal to them. Through this project, I learned about the importance of creating cohesive visuals that align with a company's branding and a user-friendly website, especially since most of the users are parents.

Working with the web development team taught me the importance of collaboration and communication in web development. It helped me understand how design decisions can impact the functionality of a website and how to work with others to achieve a common goal.

The final product was a visually stunning website that showcased the best of The Coding School's services and capabilities. I was proud to have played a part in its creation and felt that the experience had greatly enhanced my skills in web design and development.

In conclusion, the project of designing visuals and creating course bundle promotions for The Coding School was an invaluable learning opportunity that allowed me to develop my skills in web design and marketing. The experience taught me about the importance of creating cohesive visuals and developing effective marketing strategies in a real-world setting. I am grateful for the opportunity to have worked on this project and believe that it has prepared me well for future endeavors in web development.

Reflection on College Life

As a college student, I have learned a great deal about myself and the world around me. One of the most important lessons I have learned during my college life is that failure is not the end. In fact, I have learned that failure is a necessary step towards success. Each time I have failed, I have learned something new, and this has helped me become a better person.

Another thing that I have discovered during my college years is my career goals. I have come to realize that I want to work in a company that has a meaningful impact. I am passionate about making a positive difference in the world, and I want my work to reflect this. Additionally, I have discovered my love for UI/UX design, and I hope to pursue a career in this field.

During my college years, I have also discovered my personal preference for working from home. The COVID-19 pandemic has forced many of us to adapt to remote work, and I have found that I am more productive when working from home. I enjoy the flexibility and freedom that comes with working remotely, and I hope to continue this lifestyle in the future.

Finally, I have made some commitment statements for myself during college. One of my goals is to gain more skills that will help me succeed in my career. I believe that learning never ends, and I want to continue to grow and develop my skills even after I graduate.

Overall, my college life has been a time of great personal growth and discovery. I have learned so much about myself, my goals, and my preferences, and I am excited to see where these experiences will take me in the future. I am grateful for the lessons I have learned and the people I have met along the way, and I know that these memories will stay with me for a lifetime.
